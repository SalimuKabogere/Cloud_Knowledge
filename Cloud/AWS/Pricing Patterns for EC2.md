On-demand
Savings plan: Commit to consistent usage (1 or 3 years) for discounted rates. Usage beyond your commitment is billed at on-demand prices.
Reserved instances: Reserve capacity for 1 or 3 years for significant discounts (3-year gives the highest). Offers flexibility to modify Availability Zones or instance sizes within the same family
Spot Instances : Up to 90% savings by using AWS's spare capacity. Best for flexible workloads that can handle interruptions
Dedicated hosts: Physical servers fully dedicated to you. Most expensive option, but lets you use existing software licenses.
